Meet

Our Team

Luna Turner

Founder

Sarah's interest in clean water began during her travels to rural communities around the world, where she saw firsthand the struggles that people face in accessing clean water. She realized that many people lack access to safe drinking water and decided to create a solution that would be both effective and convenient.

Through PureFlow, Sarah seeks to provide a sustainable solution to the clean water crisis by creating water bottles that can filter out contaminants and make water safe to drink. Her innovative design has won awards and recognition in the industry, and the company has quickly grown to become a leader in the field.

Alex Hall

Developer

Alex's interest in clean water began during his college years, where he learned about the devastating effects of water pollution on communities around the world. As a tech-savvy individual, he realized that he could use his skills to create a platform that would raise awareness about this critical issue and inspire people to take action.

Through his work, Alex seeks to educate people about the importance of clean water and the impact that contaminated water can have on public health and the environment. He creates user-friendly websites and mobile applications that provide access to information, statistics, and news about clean water issues.

Emily Watkins

Designer

Emily's interest in clean water began early in her life, as she grew up near a polluted river and saw the effects of contaminated water firsthand. As she pursued her education in design, she realized that she could use her skills to raise awareness about this critical issue.

Through her work, Emily seeks to educate people about the importance of clean water and the impact that polluted water can have on both people and the environment. She creates striking visuals and compelling messaging that grab people's attention and inspire them to take action.
