How often do I need to replace the filter?

It depends on the level of usage and the quality of the water source. Generally, we recommend replacing the filter every 3-6 months or after filtering approximately 100 gallons of water. However, if you notice a decrease in water flow or a change in taste, it's best to replace the filter sooner.

Can I wash the water bottle in the dishwasher?

No, we do not recommend washing the water bottle or the filter in the dishwasher as the high heat can damage the filter and reduce its effectiveness. Instead, hand-wash the water bottle with warm soapy water and rinse thoroughly.

Is the water bottle BPA-free?

Yes, the water bottle is made of BPA-free plastic to ensure the safety of the user and the environment.

Can I use the water bottle filter with any type of water?

The PureFlow water bottle filter was designed to work with all use cases, It can filter dirty river or lake water; or you can use it to filter tap water. Keep in mind, the filter has an efficacy rate of 99%, so its best to try to avoid extremely dirty water with poisonous substances.

Does the water bottle filter come with a warranty?

Yes, the water bottle filter comes with a one-year limited warranty. If you encounter any issues with the product, please contact our customer service team for assistance.
